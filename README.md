## ğŸ’¡ Why PulseMind Exists

Mental health platforms today have a dangerous flaw: they throw vulnerable users into open communities immediately. Imagine someone in crisis searching for support, only to encounter cyberbullying, toxic advice, or overwhelming social pressure on their first day. Minors sign up without guardian oversight. Harmful users face no real consequences. The very place meant to heal becomes a source of harm.

**PulseMind reimagines this entirely.** We believe in **trust before exposure** and **growth before risk**. Instead of instant community access, we guide users through a carefully designed journeyâ€”starting with safe AI conversations, progressing through personalized therapy, observing community interactions safely, and finally contributing when they're truly ready.

This isn't just gatekeeping. It's **Progressive Trust**â€”a system that measures emotional readiness through real engagement, rewards positive behavior, and ensures every user enters community spaces equipped to handle them.

## ğŸ¯ How the Progressive Trust Ladder Works

At the heart of PulseMind is our **4-Phase Journey**, a dynamic system that adapts to each user's emotional readiness:

**Phase 1: AI-Only (0-10% Readiness)**  
New users start here with private, judgment-free conversations with Pulse, our AI companion powered by Google Gemini. No community access, no pressureâ€”just a safe space to express feelings and build initial trust with the platform.

**Phase 2: Micro-Therapy (10-40% Readiness)**  
After completing mood assessments and chatting consistently, users unlock our therapy library: guided meditation podcasts, yoga videos, breathing exercises, uplifting audiobooks, and mental wellness articles. They explore at their own pace, with each visit tracked to measure engagement.

**Phase 3: Community-Readonly (40-70% Readiness)**  
Once users adopt multiple therapies and maintain active engagement for several days, they can view community recommendations and eventsâ€”but not post yet. This "lurker" phase lets them observe positive interactions, understand community norms, and decide if they're ready to participate.

**Phase 4: Full-Access (70%+ Readiness)**  
Users who reach 70% readiness with zero toxicity flags earn full privileges: posting wellness tips, hosting meditation events, and chatting in real-time. Every message is filtered for toxicity, with instant blocking and reputation penalties for violations. This ensures the community stays supportive.

**The Scoring System**  
Readiness isn't arbitraryâ€”it's calculated from five factors: **30% chat session consistency**, **25% therapy adoption** (trying different tools), **20% active engagement days** (showing up matters), **15% mood stability** from assessments, and **10% community reputation** (positive contributions minus flags). The system updates in real-time, with a visual "staircase" meter showing exactly where users are and what's next.

**Why This Works**  
Traditional platforms trust everyone immediately. We trust users *when they've proven readiness*. A Phase 4 user has demonstrated emotional stability, explored coping tools, and understood community guidelinesâ€”making them far more likely to contribute positively than someone who signed up five minutes ago.

## ğŸ›¡ï¸ Safety Built Into Every Layer

**Real-Time Toxicity Prevention**  
Every community message passes through our 3-tier toxicity detector before posting. High-severity threats (self-harm language, violence) trigger instant blocks with educational feedback. Medium-severity content (bullying, harassment) reduces reputation scores by 10 points. Low-severity negativity gets warnings. Users see exactly which words were flagged, helping them learn appropriate communication. After three violations, users are shadow-bannedâ€”reverting to Phase 1 access until they rebuild trust through positive engagement.

**Guardian Consent for Minors**  
Users under 18 must provide a guardian's email during signup. The guardian receives a consent form explaining PulseMind's features and progressive access model. No minor can use the platform without verified parental approval, ensuring responsible youth mental health support.

**Visual Progress Tracking**  
The Readiness Meter isn't hiddenâ€”it's front-and-center on every user's profile. A four-step staircase shows current phase, readiness percentage, and next milestone requirements ("Complete 2 more chat sessions to unlock Micro-Therapy"). Real-time updates every 10 seconds keep users informed, while a stats dashboard breaks down their score: sessions completed, therapies adopted, active days, and reputation standing.

## ğŸ§° What You Can Do in PulseMind

PulseMind offers a comprehensive toolkit for mental wellness, with features unlocking progressively as you advance through the trust ladder:

### Chat with Pulse (Your AI Companion)
Available from day one, Pulse is a judgment-free AI therapist powered by Google Gemini 2.5 Flash. Unlike human therapists with appointment slots, Pulse responds instantly, 24/7. The conversations are context-awareâ€”Pulse remembers your concerns within each session and offers empathetic guidance. Users can even enable voice mode for text-to-speech responses, making the experience feel more personal. Every conversation increments your session count, directly boosting readiness.

### Sentiscope (Know Your Mental State)
This quick 5-question assessment evaluates your current mood through evidence-based prompts: "How often have you felt overwhelmed this week?" or "Do you find joy in activities you used to enjoy?" Sentiscope analyzes responses with Gemini Pro, generating personalized therapy recommendationsâ€”maybe audio meditation if you're anxious, or physical yoga if you're restless. Completing assessments contributes to your mood stability score, a key readiness factor. A calendar view tracks your mental health journey over weeks.

### Multi-Modal Therapy Library
Once you reach Phase 2, explore four therapy categories tailored to different needs:

- **Audio Therapy**: 12 motivational podcasts on stress management and mindfulness, plus 9 audiobooks covering topics from anxiety coping to self-compassion. Spotify embeds let you listen in-app, with personalized playlists based on Sentiscope results.

- **Physical Therapy**: Guided yoga flows for beginners and advanced practitioners, cardio exercises proven to boost mood, and 4-7-8 breathing techniques for instant calm. Video tutorials make it easy to follow along.

- **Laughing Therapy**: Because laughter is medicineâ€”curated comedy clips, feel-good sketches, and stand-up routines selected for their uplifting nature. No dark humor here, just pure joy.

- **Reading Therapy**: Articles from mental health experts, mindfulness guides by psychologists, and personal growth resources. Digestible 5-10 minute reads that educate and inspire.

Visiting each therapy type marks it "adopted," contributing 6.25 points to your readiness score per type. The system encourages explorationâ€”users who try multiple therapies advance faster.

### Safe Community Access (Phase 4 Only)
Earn 70% readiness and you unlock the heart of PulseMind: the community. Post wellness recommendations ("This meditation app changed my life!"), host virtual events (group yoga sessions via Zoom links), and chat in real-time with other Full-Access users. Every interaction is toxicity-filtered. Positive contributions earn reputation points; negativity loses them. The community is small by designâ€”only users who've proven readinessâ€”creating a genuinely supportive environment.

### Professional Support Directory
Not everything can be solved with AI or peer support. PulseMind connects you with licensed therapists and doctors through an appointment booking system. Think of it as a bridge between self-care and professional care, all in one platform.

## ğŸ› ï¸ Technology Behind PulseMind

We built PulseMind with modern, scalable technologies to ensure fast, reliable mental health support:

**Frontend Architecture**  
React 18 with TypeScript provides type-safe component architecture, while Vite powers lightning-fast development with hot module replacement. Tailwind CSS handles responsive styling, and Framer Motion adds smooth phase transition animations. React Router manages client-side navigation, Axios handles API calls, and Vanta.js creates calming 3D backgrounds. Every component is optimized for performance and accessibility.

**Backend Infrastructure**  
Node.js with Express runs our RESTful API server, connecting to MongoDB Atlas (cloud database) via Mongoose ODM. JWT tokens provide stateless authenticationâ€”no session storage means infinite scalability. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt (10 salt rounds). Google OAuth2 handles social login, reducing friction for new users. The entire backend is designed for horizontal scaling when user load grows.

**AI Integration**  
Google Gemini 2.5 Flash powers Chat with Pulse, chosen for its low latency and empathetic response generation. Gemini Pro analyzes Sentiscope assessments, providing clinically-informed therapy recommendations. Both models run server-side to protect API keys, with responses streamed to clients in real-time.

**Security & Privacy**  
All API endpoints are protected with JWT verification middleware. Passwords never leave the server in plaintext. MongoDB connections use TLS encryption. CORS is configured to allow only trusted origins (localhost for development, whitelisted production domains). Environment variables store secrets, never hard-coded. The toxicity detector runs server-side before saving any community content.

## ğŸš€ Quick Start Guide

### Prerequisites
- Node.js v18+ ([Download](https://nodejs.org/))
- MongoDB Atlas account ([Sign up free](https://www.mongodb.com/cloud/atlas))
- Google Cloud account for OAuth ([Console](https://console.cloud.google.com/))

### Installation

**Step 1: Clone and Install Dependencies**
```bash
git clone https://github.com/IEEE-Hackathon-Anveshan/PulsemindAi.git
cd PulsemindAi
npm install
```

**Step 2: Configure Environment**
```bash
cp .env.example .env
# Edit .env with your credentials (see SETUP.md for detailed guide)
```

Required environment variables:
- `VITE_GOOGLE_AI_API_KEY`: Gemini API key from Google AI Studio
- `VITE_GOOGLE_OAUTH_CLIENT_ID`: OAuth client ID from Google Cloud Console
- `GOOGLE_OAUTH_CLIENT_SECRET`: OAuth client secret
- `MONGODB_URI`: MongoDB Atlas connection string
- `JWT_SECRET`: Generate with `node -e "console.log(require('crypto').randomBytes(32).toString('hex'))"`
- `PORT`: Backend port (default 5000)

**Step 3: Start Both Servers**
```bash
# Terminal 1: Backend (port 5000)
node server/server.js

# Terminal 2: Frontend (port 5173)
npm run dev
```

**Step 4: Access PulseMind**
- Frontend: http://localhost:5173
- Backend API: http://localhost:5000

## ğŸ¬ User Journey Example

Here's how a typical new user experiences the Progressive Trust Ladder:

**Day 1: Sign Up and AI-Only Phase**  
Emma creates an account with her email. She's 17, so she enters her mom's email for guardian consent. Her mom receives the consent form and approves within minutes. Emma's profile shows "Phase 1: AI-Only" with 0% readiness. She clicks "Chat with Pulse" and shares her anxiety about upcoming exams. Pulse responds empathetically, asking follow-up questions. After a 10-minute conversation, Emma feels heard. Her readiness updates to 5% (1 session completed). The meter says: "Next milestone: Complete Sentiscope assessment."

**Day 2: Sentiscope and Phase 2 Unlock**  
Emma returns and completes the Sentiscope assessment, answering questions about her sleep quality and stress levels. Gemini Pro analyzes her responses and recommends Audio Therapyâ€”specifically, a 10-minute meditation podcast. Emma's readiness jumps to 15%, unlocking Phase 2: Micro-Therapy. She explores the therapy library, listening to two podcasts and trying a breathing exercise video. Each adoption adds 6.25 points. Her readiness is now 27.5%.

**Day 4: Phase 3 and Community Observation**  
After three active days of chatting with Pulse, trying Physical Therapy yoga flows, and completing two more Sentiscope assessments, Emma reaches 45% readiness. Phase 3: Community-Readonly unlocks. She browses the Recommendations Board, seeing posts like "This app helped me manage panic attacks" and "Weekly meditation group every Saturday." She can't post yet, but she's learning what the community values. Her engagement days score increases daily.

**Day 10: Full-Access Earned**  
Emma's been consistentâ€”6 chat sessions, 3 therapies adopted, 10 active days, strong mood stability, and 10 reputation points (default, no violations). Her readiness hits 72%. Phase 4: Full-Access unlocks! She posts her first recommendation: "Pulse helped me through exam stressâ€”talk to it if you're overwhelmed!" Other users like her post, earning her reputation. She joins a community event: a virtual meditation group. Emma feels safe here because everyone in Phase 4 has walked the same journey.

This progression took 10 days, but users can go faster or slower depending on engagement. The key: no one is rushed, and everyone who reaches Full-Access has proven they're ready.
